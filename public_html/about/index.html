Professional Experience

I am an extremely passionate Product Designer focused on designing world-class user experiences. I have worked at MikMak, LogMeIn, and DataXu.

I have been passionately applying UX principles to interactive websites and products for over 12 years on cross-functional, highly collaborative teams. I strive to strategically add value to every experience I work on by listening to customers, utilizing research, and designing with the latest tools.

ERG Leadership

I led the Mental Health Coalition ERG at MikMak for nearly 2 years, organizing events, creating content, and advocating for mental health. I'm an avid reader and mindfulness practitioner, which fuels my passion for mental health. In my free time, I enjoy making music, playing with my cats, and tinkering with home automation.

Side Projects and Hobbies

In 2017, I started designing posters on my iPad, leading to Posts for Pause.

In 2023, I wrote an article on Medium to share my thoughts. Writing has been a great way to connect with others and express my ideas.

After years of tinkering with GarageBand, I launched a YouTube channel in 2024 to share music for focus and productivity. Combining my passion for music and productivity has been wonderful.

In 2024, I also created my first Gumroad Shop to share Notion templates and music. It's rewarding to see others benefit from the tools and resources I've created.
